Ampersand does the WINDING (copper wire onto cores). Suppliers like Yori (Honger Tech) and Lu (Ualloy) provide the finished magnetic cores (annealed, epoxy coated). NOT tape-wound. Ampersand then winds copper wire around them to make finished inductors for customers like NK Technologies.

**Why:** Joe corrected me for confusing what the supplier does vs what Ampersand does.

**How to apply:** Never say a supplier is "winding cores" for Ampersand. The suppliers create/manufacture the cores. Ampersand winds. This is Ampersand's core business.
